Hi, I'm Steve. I love DIY-ing things, and creating this blog site was part of it. There's always a big joy in understanding how things work (at the cost of poor initial efficiency in making everything from the scratch, of course 🥲).

Learning has alway been a huge driving energy in my life. And sharing it with people with the matching energy is a bless. I had the luxury of being surrounded by such people when I was a Ph.D. student, but it's not been easy to regenerate it in workplaces - other goals take over your priority list at work. So instead, I started writing.

Hope these personal notes on math and computer science eventually become useful to you all. It is certainly helping me refreshing my old memories back in school. 🙂

I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Michigan State University, specializing in algebraic geometry, arithmetic geometry and number theory. Before that, I had a mixed experience of working and studying as a stat and biz major. Data science and mathematics have been a big part of my professional career since then.

Feel free to contact me with anything, I'd love to hear your thoughts. ☕️
