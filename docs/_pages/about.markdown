Hi, I'm Steve. I love DIY-ing things, and creating this blog site was part of it. There's always a big joy in understanding how things work (at the cost of miserable efficiency in making everything from the scratch, of course).

Learning has alway been a huge passion in my life. One day, at work, I missed my teaching days as a graduate TA so much when I doing my Ph.D program in mathematics. Quitting the current job not being a wise option to scratch the itch, I thought, 'I should write something to keep me motivated!'

I hope this personal eventually becomes useful to you all. It is certainly helping me refreshing my good old memories as a student. 🙂

I studied math at Michigan State University for a phd, specializing in algebraic geometry, arithmetic geometry and number theory. Before that, I had a mixed experience of working and studying as a stat and biz major. Data science and mathematics have been a big part of my professional career since then.

Feel free to contact me with anything, I'd love to hear your thoughts. ☕️
