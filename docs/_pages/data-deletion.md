## Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to have your data removed from our system, or if you want to deactivate your account on our website, follow these instructions:

1. Contact us via [our source page](https://github.com/leesugil/leesugil.github.io/tree/gh-pages) with a request for data deletion.
2. Provide sufficient information that allows us to verify your identity and locate your data in our system.
3. Once your identity is verified, we will proceed with the data deletion request and confirm once it is completed.

Please note that in some cases, we may be unable to delete your data for legal or regulatory reasons, which will be notified to you, if applicable.
